Whistle
Whistle is a dog-tracking device with a paired application. Whistle came to Collective Ray when they were still creating hardware prototypes for tracking daily activity. Together, we helped shape the future of dog wellness.
The main goal was to make checking on your dog during the day quick and pleasant. At this stage, we used Flinto for quick feedback from the client.
When we arrived at a solid place with our structure, layout, and data visualizations, it was time to explore visual direction.
After some iteration, our work was finalized and the first version of Whistle was created. Now everyone can keep track of their dog!
