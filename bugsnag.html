Bugsnag
At Bugsnag, I explored the intersection of design and engineering. Learning how to create usable prototypes with React, Javascript, and SCSS was a great addition to my repertoire.
Bugsnag is an error monitoring dashboard that shows you what's wrong with your application. With Bugsnag's filter, you can create alerts to keep track of specific errors.
My favorite project at Bugsnag was working on Settings 2.0. During that time, my main responsibilities were managing concept art and the layout of the Settings page.
When Bugsnag traveled to a convention, I supplied designs for lapel badges.
Settings 2.0 Iconography
This icon set takes a lot of inspiration from IBM's design sensibilities in the 1980s. Making clear and effective metaphors with simple styling was my goal.
